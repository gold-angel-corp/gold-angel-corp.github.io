HISTORY


SATORI is a isometric RPG that happens in ancient Japan in the year of 777. You are Nuru an Assassin that tried to kill a Monk but heard the buddha voice and decide stop kill people. But there are demons spread in the world that you must defeat, your own demons and the three Demon Kings.
