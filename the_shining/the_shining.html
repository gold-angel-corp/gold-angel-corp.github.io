Discover the mysteries about alchemy in this game where you need to find a cure for the King Baron and face challenger creatures. On this quest you are Marie Andalusia, an alchemist that need to travel to another worlds to find a not just a cure for the king but learn more about alchemy finding the scripts from The Shining. This game is in development.

Marie got a new partner on her journey. When you forward in the game you got more weapons and learn magic! Use to defeat the enemies that you encounter when travel the worlds!

The Dragon world is too many light years in technology in relation with the other worlds! Explore this final world and maybe you will find one of those scripts from The Shining!
